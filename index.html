Welcome to ConUPlugs!

What's ConUPlugs?

ConUPlugs is a platform for Concordia students used to search information about their courses. We provide information such as description of courses, ratings of teachers & resources such as past midterms.

How to use it?

Simply type in the search bar the course you are looking for. We currently provide Engineering & Computer Science courses.
